Chicken Dumpling Soup

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp. olive oil
  • ground ginger
  • cumin
  • taragon
  • fresh rosemary
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 chopped yellow onion
  • 1 chicken breast
  • 2 stalks chopped celery
  • 2 chopped carrots
  • 5 tbsp. salted butter
  • 48 oz. chicken broth
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp. baking powder

Directions

  1. In a large pot over medium heat, add the olive oil, chicken breast, ginger, taragon, and cumin.
  2. Once the chicken is cooked through, remove from the pot and the add onion, celery, and carrots to the pot.
  3. Once the onions are translucent, add the garlic and butter.
  4. Add the chopped chicken back into the pot, along with the chicken broth, 1/2 cup heavy cream, rosemary, and bay leaves. Bring to a simmer.
  5. To make the dumplings, mix the flour, rest of heavy cream, and baking power in a bowl with a rubber spatula. Form dough balls.
  6. Remove the bay leaves and rosemary from the pot, add in the dumplings, and cover for 10-15 minutes or until the dumplings double in size.
  7. Add salt, pepper, and spices to taste. I like to add in red pepper flakes for a kick!

Enjoy!
